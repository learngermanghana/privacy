After You Register: What Happens Next

  1. Payment confirmation: We will verify your payment.
  2. Email sent to you: You will receive an email from Learn Language Education Academy containing your Student Code and confirmation to the same email you registered with. Check spam/junk if you do not see it.
  3. Create your Falowen account: Go to www.falowen.com, open the Login area, and click Sign up.
  4. Use Student Code + Email: Sign up using your Student Code and email, then create your own unique password.
  5. Log in: After sign-up, log in with your email and new password anytime.

Need help or did not receive the email? Contact learngermanghana@gmail.com. Keep your Student Code and password secure—do not share them.

About Us

Falowen is a German learning platform by Learn Language Education Academy (LLEA) in Ghana. We help learners from A1 to B2 build real-world skills through structured practice, including preparation for internationally recognized proficiency levels, such as **B2**.

Our Story

Founder: Felix Asadu. Credentials: International Management (IU International University, Germany), TEFL, English for Business (YALI), Goethe B2. Felix started with a few online lessons, refined a practical method (simple explanations, targeted drills, steady feedback), and grew it into an academy and an app used by learners in Ghana and beyond.

Mission & Location

  • Mission: Deliver affordable, effective German education and help students use German confidently in real life.
  • Based: Ghana, West Africa. We offer in-person classes locally and online programs worldwide.
