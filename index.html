Yes. The fee is the same for all learning modes. Whatever you choose at registration, you will be placed in a class. For each session, you may join in person, online, or via recorded lecture—you decide each time.

  • Class duration: 10 weeks (~3 months)
  • Contract access: 6 months total access to Falowen from enrollment (continues after the 10-week class ends)
  • After 6 months: Extend at 1,000 GHS/month or re-register for a new 10-week class at the then-current fee

When do I receive my Student Code?

After payment is confirmed, you will receive an email with your Student Code and next steps. Check spam/junk if you don’t see it.

How do I create my Falowen account?

Go to www.falowen.com, open Login, and click Sign up. Use your Student Code and your email, then create a unique password.

What if the registration email doesn’t arrive?

Please check spam/junk. If it’s not there, contact learngermanghana@gmail.com or message us on WhatsApp.
